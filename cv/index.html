BPO Automation Service

Worked with real estate client to establish an automated system for Broker Price Opinion, data entry.  The system generated human like reports and had a higher accept rate then an actual human propagating the information.  BPO's would take an agent anywhere from one to two hours for just the data entry aspect, and this was compounded by having to obtain real photos of the properties and hunt for comparabilities in the MLS system.  My software BPObot was able to be given a folder that contained sub-folders with all of the addresses that needed to have their comparable properties pulled and data entered, then would automatically collect the data that was required.  The user then confirms the pulled comparables for each property and can manually select others, then the system logs into the appropriate front end website, goes to the correct form for that property and automatically propagates the information into the correct inputs.  It then validates the entire form, and generates human like writen comments where requried by drawing from a library of situational statements and variables; then mixes and matches making the written statements to have an organic, human like feel that had a very low chance of ever repeating the same statements in the same order.  It would then upload the photos automatically from the sub-folder, generate a report and go on to the next property.  We never maxed out the system, but were able to produce 50 BPO reports in a time frame of one hour.  The system was so efficient, that we were able to remove the deficit of BPO order needed in the entire Greater Las Vegas Area and had my client put at the top of the list for all BPO orders.  This system was extremely successful and was well ahead of the market by years and another similar system did not appear for almost four years after our initial private launch.
